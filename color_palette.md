## Usage Guidelines

### Burnt Orange (16-1448 TCX)

**Purpose:** Motivational and dynamic

**Suggested Use:**
- Buttons that need attention (e.g., "Submit", "Start Workflow")
- Highlights for notifications or status updates indicating action required
- Progress bars to show urgency or active tasks

---

### Colonial Blue (18-4522 TCX)

**Purpose:** Stability and trust

**Suggested Use:**
- Backgrounds for sidebars, menus, or panels with settings
- Titles or headers of sections that need a sense of authority or integrity
- Icons related to decision-making or structured workflows

---

### Skywriting (13-4302 TCX)

**Purpose:** Clarity and calmness

**Suggested Use:**
- Background color for content areas where clarity is key (e.g., organized data tables, dashboards)
- Hover states on buttons to create a clean, interactive feel
- Secondary menu styling or less prominent accent features

---

### Gray Lustre (20-0016 TPM)

**Purpose:** Neutral foundation and precision

**Suggested Use:**
- Text for subheadings or less prominent information to avoid overwhelming the main content
- Dividers, borders, or subtle shadow effects for depth in layouts
- Background areas to anchor content without drawing too much attention

---

### Bright White (11-0601 TCX)

**Purpose:** Clean, intuitive, and modern

**Suggested Use:**
- Main background color for creating a minimalistic and sleek interface
- Typography for headers and body text in contrast to darker backgrounds
- Forms, inputs, or sections requiring a crisp and clean user interaction

---

> This guidance allows you to create an app interface that aligns with the principles of usability, structure, and aesthetic harmony.
