How it works

Three steps to better decisions

From idea to consensus in minutes — no prompt engineering required.

01
🎯
Define your question
State what you need decided. Strategic, technical, creative — any domain works. Add context, constraints, and the depth of analysis you need.
02
🤝
Assign your AI council
Pick 2–4 agents and assign each a distinct reasoning role: analytical, contrarian, creative, cautious. Mix models from different providers for true diversity.
03
⚡
Watch the debate unfold
Agents debate in structured rounds, challenge each other's reasoning, and converge on a synthesized verdict — complete with confidence scores and dissenting views.
